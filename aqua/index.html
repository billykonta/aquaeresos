About AQUA

Aqua is more than just a beach canteen—it's a dream brought to life by two 19-year-old friends with a passion for creating the ultimate seaside experience. With a focus on quality, refreshing flavors, and a vibrant atmosphere, we strive to offer our customers the perfect mix of relaxation and enjoyment by the water. Whether you're here for a cold drink, a delicious snack, or just good vibes under the sun, Aqua is the place to be. Join us and make every beach day unforgettable!
